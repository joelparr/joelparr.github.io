Snowball Expeditions Everest Base Camp Trek.

This incredible experience will take you from the busy city of Kathmandu and across the Himalayan Mountains to the start point of the historical route to Everest base camp. Whether you join us as a group or as an individual this expedition allows you to work together, to challenge yourselves and to do something quite extraordinary with your lives.

Everest Base Camp Trek Challenge yourself and to do something extraordinary with your life

Experience for yourself this journey of a lifetime …

… to one of the great wonders of the natural world. The Snowball Expeditions Everest Base Camp Trek is a great opportunity to see Mount Everest, to interact with the communities en-route and to immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring scenery that is the Himalayas.

  • What is the expedition itinerary?
    Day 1
    Arrival at Kathmandu airport (1400m), meet the Nepalese guides and transfer to Hotel. Overnight at Hotel Vaishali **** (four star).
    Day 2
    Preparation for the trek to Everest Base Camp – Overnight at Hotel Vaishali. Day’s sight seeing around Kathmandu.
    Day 3
    Transfer to airport to fly to Lukla (2800m). Trek to Phakdingma (2652m). Lunch en-route. Dinner and lodge.
    Day 4
    Acclimatisation day and visit to local school which is supported by Snowball Expeditions.
    Day 5
    After breakfast trek to Phakdingma – Namche Bazaar (3440m) village in the clouds. Lunch en-route. Dinner and overnight at a lodge.
    Day 6
    Acclimatization day at Namche Bazaar. A short day’s hike to visit a school funded by the Sir Edmund Hillary foundation along with the support from Snowball Expeditions. Breakfast, lunch dinner included with an over night stop at a lodge.
    Day 7
    After breakfast we trek from Namche Bazaar to the Monastery at Tengboche and the view of Everest at (3867m) Lunch on route. Dinner and overnight at a lodge.
    Day 8
    After breakfast we trek from Tengboche to Dingboche (4343m) Lunch on route. Dinner and overnight at a lodge.
    Day 9
    After breakfast we trek from Dingboche to Lobuche (4940m) Lunch on route. Dinner and overnight at a lodge.
    Day 10
    After breakfast we trek from Lobuche to Gorakshep (5288m) after lunch we hike up to Everest Base Camp and back to Gorakshep to overnight and have dinner at a lodge.
    Day 11
    Early morning excursion to Kalapattar (5545m) for a sunrise view of the Mt. Everest range. After breakfast we trek from Gorakshep to Pheriche (4250m) Lunch on route. Dinner and overnight at a lodge.
    Day 12
    After breakfast we trek back from Pheriche to Namche Bazaar. Lunch on route. Dinner and overnight a lodge.
    Day 13
    After breakfast we trek from Namche Bazaar to Lukla. Lunch on route. Dinner and overnight at a lodge.
    Day 14
    Fly to Kathmandu. Freshen up! - Overnight at the Hotel Vaishali.
    Day 15
    Kathmandu valley sightseeing. This can be done around Durbar Square, Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple) and Pashupatinath, the Hindus holiest temple. Overnight at Hotel Vaishali. Final team meal in a traditional restaurant.
    Day 16
    Final departure home.
