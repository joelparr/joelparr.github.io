Inside Social Games

Explore the World

Players visit a non-player character to see what they have for sale. The objective is to haggle to the cheapest possible price and make a purchase. Once bought, players then turn around and locate someone else that is willing to buy the item for the highest possible price.

Customize your home

Use your incoming wealth to decorate and upgrade your home from humble abode to grand mansion, buying plenty of fine art and mod cons that will have the players on your friends list positively drooling when they come to visit.
